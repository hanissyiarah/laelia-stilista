    BACKGROUND

    The store was synonymous with the youth movement and counterculture styles of the "Laelia Stilista" and it dramatically transformed the way clothes, hijab, trousers, and shoes were made, marketed, and displayed. The boutique, as a retailing concept, is associated with a distinct identity that reflects the designer's or owner's taste; small-scale production with quick turnover of merchandise; fashion novelty and experimentation; innovative displays and interiors; and an informality among owner,salespeople, and clientele

    VISION

    Be the always wanted place for stylish and great fashion products to share with family and friends.

    MISSION

    To provide designer fashion at a revolutionary price while setting a precedent for socially responsible enterprises.

    OBJECTIVE

    To provide professional education encompassing the whole range of fashion and textile activities, and to produce “all-round” graduates with vision and a global viewpoint, a sense of social responsibility, and critical and creative thinking abilities
